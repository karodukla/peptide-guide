Important Safety Warning — Active Cancer & Malignancy

Several peptides in this guide stimulate growth hormone (GH), promote cell proliferation, or induce angiogenesis (new blood vessel formation). These mechanisms, while beneficial for healthy individuals, may accelerate tumor growth or support tumor vascularization in individuals with active or recent cancer.

Affected peptides are marked with colored warning badges. If you have or have had any cancer, pre-malignant condition, or hormone-sensitive tumour — consult a qualified oncologist before using any peptide on this list. This includes but is not limited to: breast, prostate, colorectal, thyroid, and pituitary cancers.
